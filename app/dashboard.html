💛 Nutrition Tips
🥬 Eat plenty of leafy greens for folate (spinach, sukuma wiki)
🥚 Include protein at every meal — eggs, beans, groundnuts
💧 Drink 8–10 glasses of water daily
🍌 Bananas and sweet potatoes are great for potassium
🚫 Avoid raw meat, uncooked eggs and alcohol
🛌 Rest & Movement
😴 Aim for 8–9 hours of sleep — use a pillow between your knees
🚶 A 20–30 min gentle walk is safe and beneficial most days
🧘 Try gentle stretches or prenatal yoga to ease back pain
⛔ Avoid lying flat on your back after 20 weeks — sleep on your side
🗓️ Antenatal Reminders
📋 Don't miss your ANC visits — they protect you and baby
💊 Take your iron, folic acid and calcium as prescribed
🦟 Sleep under a treated mosquito net every night
📞 Keep your midwife's number saved and easy to reach
