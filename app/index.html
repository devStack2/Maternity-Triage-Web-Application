🎨 Priority levels

Know what your colour means.

Our simple four-colour system helps you understand urgency — clearly and calmly.

🔴

RED — Immediate

Heavy bleeding, seizure, BP ≥160/110, abnormal fetal heart rate. Go to emergency now.

🟠

ORANGE — Urgent

Blurred vision, reduced baby movements, suspected preterm labor. Contact your midwife today.

🟡

YELLOW — Soon

Fever, leaking fluid, mild pain, swelling. See someone within a few hours.

🟢

GREEN — All good

No urgent symptoms. You can rest and monitor. Mention it at your next visit.
